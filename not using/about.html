Start of the Lizard Life

For 125 years, The Animal Rescue League of Boston (ARL) has responded to the needs of animals and the people who care about them. In fact, we are often the first to respond.

With over 1,200 dedicated volunteers and 100 staff members, ARL lends unwavering support to local animals, families, and neighborhoods. We provide outstanding veterinary care, adoption, and field services. We also confront the root causes of animal cruelty and neglect through special police investigation and advocacy aimed at strengthening the laws that protect animals.

Since 1899, ARL has helped over 6.2 million animals in need!

Today, we increasingly focus on prevention and the impact we can have on more animals; keeping them out of shelters and in the communities where they belong. This ambitious call to action is reflected in our vision for the future.

ARL does not receive government grants or public funding and relies on the generosity of our supporters to help animals in need. We ask all those who share our goals to reaffirm a commitment to the future we all hope to achieve. Together, we can make it happen!

Hunts

At Lizard Kings Hunting Services, we provide an unparalleled hunting experience tailored to both novice and seasoned hunters. Our expert guides, equipped with years of experience and in-depth knowledge, lead you through exclusive, game-rich hunting grounds. We offer state-of-the-art equipment, ensuring you have everything you need for a successful and enjoyable hunt. Our customizable hunt packages cater to your specific preferences, whether you're pursuing big game or small game, ensuring a suitable challenge for everyone. Committed to ethical hunting practices and wildlife conservation, we educate clients on responsible techniques, promoting sustainability for future generations. Comfortable accommodations and comprehensive post-hunt services, including game processing and taxidermy referrals, ensure that your adventure is both memorable and hassle-free. Home Removel

At Lizard Kings Home Removal Services, we specialize in the safe and efficient removal of lizards, iguanas, and other reptiles from your home. Our team of trained professionals uses humane and environmentally friendly methods to capture and relocate these creatures, ensuring they are safely returned to their natural habitat. We offer prompt and reliable service, addressing infestations quickly to restore peace and comfort to your living space. Our expertise in reptile behavior and advanced removal techniques guarantees minimal disruption to your home while effectively resolving the issue.

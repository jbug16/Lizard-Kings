Start of the Lizard Life

Lizard Kings was founded in May 2021 by Cameron Kersey and Daniel Post. During a few fishing trips in the canals of Cape Coral, they noticed numerous iguanas, knowing they were invasive to Florida. After some research, Cameron and Danny discovered that hunting these pests was legal and already practiced in other parts of the state. Thus, Lizard Kings was born.

In the past three years, Lizard Kings has expanded, becoming authorized airgun dealers for several brands and launching a clothing line called "Lizard Life." Their next step is to purchase land and build a warehouse in Cape Coral, featuring a full-line airgun store and an indoor shooting range. All members of Lizard Kings are Cape Coral natives.

Co-founded by Cameron Kersey and Daniel Post, the team includes Daniel's daughter, Viviana (Viv) Post, and Hannashae Holcomb. Viviana, an original member, serves as both entertainer and iguana retriever. Captain Hannashae Holcomb joined over a year ago as a videographer/photographer. She expressed interest in more involvement and underwent the Lizard Kings Captain's Training Course, passing in three weeks. Since then, she has excelled as a junior charter captain.
